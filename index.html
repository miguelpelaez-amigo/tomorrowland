Buy your tickets

Tomorrowland Brazil takes place at Maeda Park in Itu, located in the state of São Paulo. Plan your trip in advance. Whether by Official Shuttle, own vehicle, tour or app, instructions on how to get to the Festival and DreamVille worry-free are below.

How to get to Tomorrowland Brazil

Official Shuttle.
You can easily access the Festival with the Tomorrowland Brasil Official Shuttle, the most practical way to get to and from Maeda Park, disembarking at the closest entrance to the Festival.

Shuttles to and from, departing and arriving at Holiday Inn Parque Anhembi - Rua Professor Milton Rodriguez, nº 100 and Hilton Morumbi - Av. das Nações Unidas, nº 12901.

Check boarding options and secure your place.

ACCESO AL FESTIVAL

  • Maximum Capacity

  • The different areas of the festival are subject to maximum capacity.

  • Please avoid risky situations and follow the indications of the staff. Evacuation routes and emergency exits will be clearly marked.

  • Prohibited entry

  • Your entry may be denied by the Organization. Clothing and/or other (visible) physical attributes containing discriminatory messages or promoting any form of violence will not be tolerated.

  • Minimum Age

  • The minimum age to enter the festival is 18 years old. This is a strict policy. Anyone born in 2007 or later, even if accompanied by a responsible person or carrying legal authorization, will not be allowed to enter the festival.
